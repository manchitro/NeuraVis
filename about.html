About this project

NeuraVis is the work of Md. Sazid Uddin. This is an effort to learn and teach about neural networks by implementing a simple handwriting digit recognition model. There's a plan to include an animated visual simulator of a network at work to help better understand the inner workings of how handriting recognition works.

As of now, there are some reading material available that will be important to the understanding of the simulation.

Thank you for visiting!
